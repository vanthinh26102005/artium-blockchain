2. Visit the profile edit page and click `Register as Seller`; confirm profile settings routes to `/seller/register`.
3. Confirm `/seller/register` only renders after the auth gate passes.
4. Submit seller registration once and verify the flow redirects back to profile editing after success.
5. Revisit `/seller/register` in the same session, or trigger the duplicate path for a user who already has a seller profile.
6. Confirm duplicate-registration recovery loads the existing seller profile path and shows the recovery CTA instead of offering a second registration.

- `/seller/register` remains the protected seller-registration route.
- The profile-settings CTA remains the entry path.
- Seller-profile creation still depends on backend-authenticated `POST /identity/seller-profiles`.
- `isVerified` stays false by default, and payment onboarding remains incomplete by default.
