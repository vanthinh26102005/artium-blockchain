| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-19-04 | Spoofing | `SellerAuctionTermsPreview` | mitigate | Preview copy must say `Starts when activated` and `Activation handled in next step`, not live/started/confirmed. |
